I have always had a passion for art in all forms from an early age, which included drawing, painting, sculpture, music and nearly anything creative. That passion now includes all forms of media, with a focus on graphic design and Web developing.

I received a bachelor's degree in magazine journalism with a minor in graphic arts technology from Ball State University in 2007. During my time at Ball State, I gained experience with photography, writing, researching, graphic design and Web developing.

Near the end of my time at Ball State, my passion for graphic design and the Web grew while the journalism marketplace shrank. That led me to pursue a degree in Web design and interactive media at the Art Institute of Pittsburgh.

While attending the Art Institute, I have expanded my understanding of graphic design and Web developing. I have learned the principles of design for layout, typography and color. I have also gained a strong understanding of how to plan a Web site for a target audience, current Web design trends, and several Web languages.

If you are looking for someone that can create nearly any type of media, then you have come to the right place.
